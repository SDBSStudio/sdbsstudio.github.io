About Us

History

SDBS Studio Development is the new name for Qonz Network, which was founded in 2020. It originally focused on Minecraft servers, but by the end of 2022, it rebranded as SDBS Studio Development and shifted its focus to Roblox. The name SDBS is derived from the first names of four close friends of Qonz, and when interpreted, it stands for "Software-Based Digital Simulation."
